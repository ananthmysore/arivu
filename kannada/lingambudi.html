Lingambudhi Bird Sanctuary

Caring for Environment.

As Arivu is hosted next to Lingambudhi bird sanctuary, we do our best to support conservation and development of the bird sanctuary. Lingabmudhi forest has been declared as a protected forest by Karnataka forest departement.please visit this link to know more - http://www.mysorenature.org/lingambudhi

Dr. Manohara who is one of the founder, trustee and resource person for Arivu and Mr. Berty Olivera who is a resource person and trust member for Arivu are members of the Lingambudhi forest committee, constituted by karnataka forest department to protect and conserve the Lingambudhi forest.For more information please visit http://www.mysorenature.org/lingambudhi/lingambudhi-lake-and-environ
