Volunteer

Arivu is entirely a collective initiative. We are what we are only because of all our well wishers and supporters.

Thank you for considering Arivu for volunteering. It doesn’t matter if you are in Mysore or anywhere else in India, we welcome Volunteers from all walks of life to share their learnings with us and to learn from what we do here.

Volunteering at Arivu School could be a meaningful learning experience. You can be a part of a truly radical approach in education and community developed. We require volunteers in following areas

  • Teaching – all subjects (activity based, game based, multimedia based)
  • Web designing (to help us with our website, blog, fb)
  • Designing and Art (to help us improve aesthetics of our campus, website, hearts)
  • Curriculum / content developing
  • Activity designing
  • Nature
  • Teacher Training

This is just an indicative list. If you can’t seem to fit anywhere, just write to us. We will find something together.

Please write to us with your details to discuss the opportunities further. Thank you ! (info@arivu.org)
