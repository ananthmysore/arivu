Contribute

Arivu is entirely a collective initiative. We are what we are only because of all our well wishers and supporters.

Thank you for considering your kind support to our endeavor. Arivu, from the very beginning has been work of collaboration, it started with ideas contributed by many and in a farm contributed by our friend Sri Raghavendra. Later with help of other friends, trust members and trustee, we were able to purchase a small land where our campus is now located. We have been and will continue to be a collective and community initiative. Your contributions not only help us grow but also encourage us to keep up our work.

We strive to be sustainable in our operations but sometimes we do need support to take to newer avenues. We would be very grateful to receive your support in all forms. You can make monetary donations or donate us something we need like books, study material, learning toys. Please write to us and we can share with you our current requirements.
