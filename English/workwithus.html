Work With Us

Arivu is entirely a collective initiative. We are what we are only because of all our well wishers and supporters.

Thank you for your interest in working with Arivu. We are always welcoming of passionate people to expand our family. Below are some of our current requirements

Subject Teachers (1st grade to 5th grade)

We are looking for teachers for all subjects. People who can handle multiple subjects will be better suited. Except for English, other subject teachers must either be able to speak and understand Kannada or willing to learn.
Qualifications and experience do not really matter. If you’ve some, great ! If you don’t, even better !

Resource Persons (Science, Language, Environment, Culture and Mathematics)

Arivu resource centre helps Arivu school as well others to learn and teach. We are looking for resource persons for various subjects to take care of resource centre operations.
The resource person will be responsible for maintaining, demonstrating, upgrading and training. He/She will inspire and train teachers and students to use the resources as a tool for learning.
Qualification and experience no bar !

Others

You can’t seem to find a role for yourself, but still feels like joining hands ? Great ! Just write to us or better plan a visit. We believe in serendipity !

Please write to us or plan a visit to discuss the opportunities further. (info@arivu.org)
